About Us

Hi, we are Nerissa & Simone, a mom duo who both love afternoon tea. Simone, a pastry chef with a passion for photography & decor. Nerissa, financial background & wedding decorating experience with a passion for all things creative, from cakes to decor. Our decision to pursue this venture was based on our unique creative styles which compliment each other, making us the perfect team.


Le Gouter LLC will offer Luxury Tea Picnics by setting up our pop-up tea service at your chosen venue; primarily parks, beaches, homes and back-yards. We prepare and create beautiful tablescapes with a mix of floral, vintage and modern aesthetics with the options for traditional tea menu(sandwiches, scones, desserts paired with a fresh selection of teas), charcuterie boards, optional cakes for your next special occasion at any selected location.


We want to constantly perfect ourselves in providing the ultimate luxury tea party with a twist(whimsical and magical) allowing us to use our creativity and talents to provide the best experience for our guests
